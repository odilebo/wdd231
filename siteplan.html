DJI Drone Technology - Site Plan

Site Name

DJI Drone Technology – Showcases products, services, and information about DJI drones.

Optional domain availability: djidrone.co.za

Site Purpose

The site serves as a hub for DJI drone customers and enthusiasts, allowing users to browse products, view technical specs, request demos, and contact support. It also highlights featured members in the drone community.

Scenarios

  • Which DJI drone is best for aerial photography and videography?
  • How can I request a demo or technical support for my DJI drone?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #0077cc – headings, buttons, nav links.
  • Secondary Color: #f6f9fc – background.
  • Accent Color: #ff9900 – highlights, call-to-action buttons.

Typography

  • Lobster: headings (h1, h2, h3)
  • Arial, sans-serif: body text, paragraphs, lists
